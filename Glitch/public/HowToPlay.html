1v1

Go to Play Now, select 1v1, and wait for an opponent

Use your right hand to make rock, paper, or scissors

Hold up your pick until the timer runs out, then confirm or redo your pick

After the game finishes, you can play against the same opponent or leave

King Of The Hill

Go to Play Now, select KOTH, and wait for your turn

The King is at the top of the hill, to take his throne you must beat him

The King gets to keep playing as long as he wins and takes on any opponent thrown his way

The loser gets kicked out and leaves the crown for the King
